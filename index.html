Welcome to Metz theolab !

Website of the Bible studies department of the University of Lorraine !

SCRIBES project

Development of a Web application for easy textual criticism, leveraging recent advances in automatic Natural Language Processing.

Funded by Biblissima+.

Stay tuned on our Github

BENTO project

A two years project on the analysis of the variants of the Hebrew Ben Sira manuscripts.

Funded by the Lorraine University of Excellence.

Check out the complete XML transcription of Ben Sira

SHERBET project

A four years project on the implementation of computational stemmatology algorithms and their application to the transmission of the Hebrew Bible.

Check out the stemmabench
